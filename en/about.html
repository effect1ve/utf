Features and benefits

Various material options, including a mixture of synthetic materials (80/20), fully synthetic and specially treated materials for various operating conditions.
Purification classes F8, F9, E11 and E12 provide high filtration efficiency for various applications and air pollution levels.
Excellent corrugation technology with constant geometry and the same distance between the folds ensures laminar flow throughout the life of the filter.
Galvanized and stainless steel end caps provide additional protection and durability of the filter design.
Guaranteed low average pressure drop reduces energy consumption and ensures more efficient operation of the ventilation system.
A long service life compared to analogues from other manufacturers allows you to significantly reduce the cost of maintenance and replacement of filters.
The high maximum pressure drop (rupture) – up to 5000 Pa ensures reliable operation of the filter even under extreme operating conditions.
