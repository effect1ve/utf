“FROM” SERIES

Filter elements of "FROM" series are designed for cleaning from solid mechanical impurities of industrial oils in mechanisms of industrial equipment.

They are used in stationary single element and multielement filters and low-pressure oil stations.

Nominal filtration fineness for mechanical impurities 10, 25, 40 µm
Moisture resistance 80%
Pressure difference on the clean element 0,02 - 0,05 MPa
Recommended final difference 0,25 MPa
Maximum difference in pressure up to 2 MPa
Heat resistance up to +100 °C
Operating temperature up to +80 °C
Transportation and storage 3(ZhZ) acc. to GOST 15150
