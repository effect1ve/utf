“PFK” SERIES

Filter elements of "PFK" series are air panel filters of prepurification class G4. They are designed for air purification of ventilation systems, air preparation systems of gas turbine units (GTU) and gas compressor units (GCU).

Housing is made of corrosion-resistant steel for structural rigidity and easy disposal or recycling.

Class of purification G4 - M5
Recommended pre-purification class labyrinth inlet
Moisture resistance 100%
Pressure difference on the clean element not more than 120 Pa
Recommended final difference 250 Pa
Efficiency up to 4250 m3/h
Operating temperature up to +100 °C
Transportation and storage 3(ZhZ) acc. to GOST 15150
